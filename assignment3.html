Sweet Potato Bread


Sweet potato bread is a type of moist bread made from mashed sweet potatoes. It is often a sweet, cake-like quick bread similar to banana bread or pumpkin bread. The sweet potato can be cooked and softened before being used or simply baked with the bread; boiling and pureeing is the most common approach. Additional ingredients include chocolate chips, maple syrup, brown sugar, or nuts (such as walnuts).

Sweet potato bread is usually baked in a rectangular loaf pan and is often made in late fall when sweet potatoes are more available.

History

Potatoes existed once and people were like woah, why don't we mash some and put it in this bread, and voila, potato bread. After that they're like oh, what if we mashed sweet potatoes and put them in this bread. Voila! Sweet potato bread. Great with chocolate chips, cinnamon, or brown sugar.
