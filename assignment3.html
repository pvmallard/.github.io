Sweet Potato Bread


 Type: Sweet Bread, Quick Bread

 Place of Origin: Native Americans

 Key Ingredients: Sweet potatoes, flour, sugar

Sweet potato bread is a type of moist bread made from mashed sweet potatoes. It is often a sweet, cake-like quick bread similar to banana bread or pumpkin bread. The sweet potato can be cooked and softened before being used or simply baked with the bread; boiling and pureeing is the most common approach. Additional ingredients include chocolate chips, maple syrup, brown sugar, or nuts (such as walnuts).

Sweet potato bread is usually baked in a rectangular loaf pan and is often made in late fall when sweet potatoes are more available.

History

Potatoes existed once and people were like woah, why don't we mash some and put it in this bread, and voila, potato bread. After that they're like oh, what if we mashed sweet potatoes and put them in this bread. Voila! Sweet potato bread. Great with chocolate chips, cinnamon, or brown sugar. Allergy Friendly Recipes

Due to the moisture from the sweet potatoes, this is an excellent type of sweet bread to modify for dietary restrictions like allergies or intolerances.

Not all recipes come with all dietary restrictions in mind, often recipes will need to be adjusted based on altitude, ingredients on hand, and any restricitons or preferences. Often, people will find it easier to take regular recipes and substitue the needed ingredients rather than finding a uniquely vegan or gluten-free recipe, though it depends on the baker.

Look here for some helpful ingredient subsitutions. Otherwise, common subsitutions are using non-dairy alternatives for milk or butter, or subsituing eggs for flaxseed or banana.
