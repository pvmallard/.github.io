History

Potatoes existed once and people were like woah, why don't we mash some and put it in this bread, and voila, potato bread. After that they're like oh, what if we mashed sweet potatoes and put them in this bread. Voila! Sweet potato bread. Great with chocolate chips, cinnamon, or brown sugar. I've heard walnuts too but only heathens eat walnuts.
